Splice Sounds Shortcuts

  • Shift + P

    Plays the currently active sample

  • Shift + S

    Stops the currently playing sample

  • Shift + L

    Likes the currently active sample

  • Shift + A

    Selects the currently active sample

  • Shift + D

    Downloads the currently active sample

  • Shift + Alt + D

    Downloads all selected samples

  • Shift + Alt + A

    Deselects all selected samples

What is an active sample?

When you click on a sample in splice sounds it starts playing and becomes active. It will remain active until you navigate away using the up/down arrows on your keyboard.
